Drift A Lot

About

Small arcade game in wich the player drifts a car to escape the police. It was made using Unity.

The objective of the project wasn't creating a fun game, but rather tackle the workflow used for publishing a mobile game in the Play Store. It even contains ads (I personally dislike ads, but this was for learning purposes and I'm not expecting to make a single cent out of it).

Team

Artists: Me

Contribution

I programed the player movement, items, effects, menus, score, sounds, ui, integrated the ads and uploaded the game to the Play Store (it's in revision right now). I don't remember if the AI used is the one I programmed or the one my teammate did.
