Project Orion

About

Shoot 'Em Up game where the player steers a ship through a circuit and shoots at the enemies. The engine was built from the ground up using C++ and OpenGL.

Team

Programmers: Benat Morisset, Emilia Paredes, Inaki Jimenez, Javier Tafur, Marc Mirapeix, Pablo Marcos, Roberto Acea
Artists: Me?
Musician: Jorge Damien Fernandez

Contribution

Engine: Graphics (particle system was done by Javier Tafur, I just did the rendering part).
Gameplay: The level bounds (so that the player doesn't leave the level), other small details (can't remember right now).
Art: Models, particles and effects (using shaders).
